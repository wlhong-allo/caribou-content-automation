<h1>Top 10 Family-Friendly Hotels in Ubud, Bali: Luxury & Budget Stays for Aussie Families (2025)</h1>

<p>Nestled in the lush highlands of central Bali, Ubud is the cultural heart of the island and a favourite escape for Australian families seeking both relaxation and rich cultural experiences. Whether you're after a luxury escape with all the bells and whistles or a budget-friendly stay that doesn’t compromise on charm, Ubud has something for everyone. In this guide, we've handpicked the top 10 family-friendly hotels in Ubud for 2025 — all perfect for families travelling with kids.</p>

<h2>Bisma Eight Ubud</h2>
<p><a href="https://www.agoda.com/en-gb/bisma-eight-ubud/hotel/bali-id.html" target="_blank">Bisma Eight</a> is a sophisticated blend of modern design and tropical living, just minutes from Ubud’s centre. Aussie families will appreciate its spacious suites, incredible on-site dining, and proximity to Ubud Palace and Monkey Forest.</p>
